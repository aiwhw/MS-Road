================================================================================
Data Format
================================================================================

All images are provided in NumPy (.npy) format:
- Shape: (8, 1200, 900)  [Channels, Width, Height]
- 8 spectral bands: 395-950 nm
- Resolution: 1200 x 900 pixels

For usage examples, please refer to the README.md and examples/ directory.
